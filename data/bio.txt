R.M.~Grassmann is a Ph.D. Candidate with the Department of Computer Science and a research assistant with the Continuum Robotics Laboratory at the University of Toronto, Canada.
He works on continuum robotics and machine learning.
From 2018 to 2019 he worked as a research assistant at the Laboratory for Continuum Robotics at the Leibniz University Hannover.
Before all that, he completed a B.Sc. and a M.Sc. Degree in Mechatronics at the Leibniz University Hannover, Germany in 2016 and 2018, respectively.
For his Master's thesis, he was awarded the Honoree of the NiedersachsenMetall Foundation.
